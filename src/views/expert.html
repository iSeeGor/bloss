Sophie is Registered Nutritionist (RNutr) with a BSc Psychology degree, an MSc in Clinical Nutrition, founder of Sophie’s Healthy Kitchen, co-host of the Forking Wellness podcast and co-author of recently released nutrition book ‘Forking Wellness’.

Her non diet approach to nutrition sees her work with individuals to develop a healthy relationship with food as well as specialising in pre and post natal nutrition.

Sophie has worked with leading brands in the food industry and also works with clients in a 1-1 setting at the Rhitrition clinic on Harley St.
